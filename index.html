Wild Yeast Culture

Growing Life from Flour & Water

Nadine's wild yeast starter has been lovingly maintained for over two years. This living culture captures the unique microflora of the Isle of Lewis, giving every loaf its distinctive flavor and character.

Unlike commercial yeast, wild yeast creates complex flavors through slow fermentation, resulting in bread that's easier to digest and incredibly flavorful.

Slow Fermentation Magic

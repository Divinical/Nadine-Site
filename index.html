Living Wild Yeast Culture

Growing Life from Flour & Water

Nadine's wild yeast starter has been lovingly maintained for over two years, capturing the unique microflora of the Isle of Lewis and giving every loaf its distinctive character.

Unlike commercial yeast, this living culture creates complex flavors through slow fermentation, resulting in bread that's easier to digest and incredibly flavorful.
